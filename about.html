Adam Rhoades is from the East Midlands of the UK and currently lives in Lincoln. He developed an interest in photography during a career break in 2006, and has been working in the field since 2007.

Adam works professionally for a leading online jewellery retailer, providing in-house product and point-of-sale photographic work. Adam has also worked with many arts organisations in the Midlands, including the Nomad Trust, Kodak Express, Orgasmic Caf√© Bar and Lava PR.

Outside of professional commissions, Adam has a particular interest in portraiture, regularly undertaking personal projects and shooting portraits of individuals and groups.

Adam's work has been exhibited in the Czimmerl Studio and Gallery at St Martins, amongst others, and is regularly displayed in Coffee Aroma, Lincoln.

For information, enquiries and bookings, contact Adam directly: adam@adamrhoades.com.
